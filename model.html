Kolli Hills, Namakkal District. It is located in the Namakkal, in mid west Tamil Nadu. It is usually not as crowded as the more famous hill stations of Tamil Nadu, no gardens, hotels or restaurants like in Ooty or Kodaikkanal or Yercaud, but Kolli malai has its own charm.

The Hindu temples and monuments at Mamallapuram have become famous tourist attractions. One of the many festivals in Tamil Nadu is the Koovagam festival, where thousands of people gather to celebrate their transgender identities. In Tamil Nadu Bharata natyam, Karnatak music, and movies are all widely popular..
